Wind Speed

From the City Latitude vs Wind Speed graph, we can see that most cities around the equator (0 degrees) have lower wind speeds, about 0-15 mph, and cities toward the north, especially between 40-60 degrees latitude, have higher wind speeds. This may be due to the fact that there are many high mountains within those latitudes as well as temperate grasslands, where there are no trees to break strong winds.
